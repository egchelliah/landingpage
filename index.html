hey there! welcome to just a carpenter. we're a husband and wife duo that love woodworking. we've made all kinds of things over the years, but recently have made some beautiful cutting boards for friends and family. hope you enjoy our work.

we rip kiln dried hardwood, and use food safe, water proof glue to begin our boards.
we then use handplanes and chisels to remove the excess glue
we draw out our shapes on the glue up
we then cut our shapes using the bandsaw

once the building process is done, we spend hours adding the finishing touches. the final shapes are planed, and sanded to a buttery smooth grit. here are some boards that we have made.
